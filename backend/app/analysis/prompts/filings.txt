{
  "revenue_trend": "<increasing | stable | decreasing | not_available>",
  "margin_analysis": "<brief description of gross/operating/net margin trends>",
  "risk_changes": "<summary of new or changed risk factors compared to typical filings, note any unusual items>",
  "guidance_sentiment": <float from -1.0 (very negative outlook) to 1.0 (very positive outlook), or null if no forward guidance>,
  "key_findings": [
    "<finding 1: most important insight>",
    "<finding 2>",
    "<finding 3>",
    "<finding 4>",
    "<finding 5>"
  ]
}

Guidelines:
- Focus on CHANGES and SURPRISES rather than routine disclosures
- For 10-K/10-Q: compare language about risks, revenue drivers, and outlook to standard boilerplate
- For 8-K: identify the material event and assess its significance
- Revenue trend: look for YoY and QoQ comparisons in Management Discussion & Analysis
- Risk changes: highlight NEW risk factors or significantly reworded existing ones
- Guidance sentiment: -1.0 if company is warning about headwinds, +1.0 if raising outlook
- Key findings: list the 5 most important takeaways an investor should know
- If the filing is truncated and you cannot determine a field, set it to null or "not_available"
- Be factual and specific — cite numbers when available

Respond ONLY with valid JSON. No commentary before or after.
